We love food. We've both grown up in California where we are lucky to have diverse foods of many cuisines. Our comfort foods are mostly Asian foods, but we love different trying new things, and we love pizza! We have friends and family all over the Bay Area, so we drive around trying different restaurants. You can find some of our favorites here - whenever anyone visits from out of town, you can be sure we'll be bringing them to these places.
