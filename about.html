About Us

We are a young couple living in the Bay Area and we love to eat! During the COVID-19 pandemic we started to experiment with various recipes and loved it. We particularly love East Asian and Southeast Asian food and flavors, but have also tried making Mediterranean, South Asian, and Latin American recipes. We'd love to keep expanding our palette and skills and this is to help us track our favorites.

Cooking

We've tried a few different recipes. Some of our regularly made food meals are kimchi fried rice, veggie fried rice, spaghetti, baked salmon, smashed cucumber, Vietnamese spring rolls and various tofu/vegetable stir-fries. Zack is a fan of making really flavorful sauces whereas Crystal sticks more to original flavors of the food items. We both love spicy!

Some of our other favorite things that we've made and want to make more regularly:

  • Taco bowls
  • Paneer tikka masala
  • Butter garlic mushroom pizza
  • Japchae
  • Kimchi
  • Sushi
  • Butternut squash chili
  • Thai tofu/vegetable yellow curry
  • creamy pumpkin pasta

Baking

Crystal does more of the baking, but Zack is always more than happy to help taste test and enjoys the baked goods. Crystal has experimented with bread (including sourdough - which somehow got progressively worse with each trial??), cookies, butter mochi, challah, Japanese bread, bagels, and cakes. We like to watch the Great British Baking Show and are impressed by the beautiful bakes and designs on the show!

Some things we (Crystal) hope to learn to make:

  • Panna cotta
  • Japanese shokupan
  • Japanese strawberry shortcake
  • Cheesecake
  • Hot cross buns (a family friend makes amazing ones)
  • Red bean buns
  • Taiwanese Pineapple cakes
  • Tiramisu
